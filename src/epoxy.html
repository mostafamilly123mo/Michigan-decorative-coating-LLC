Metallic Epoxy

Installation steps
  • We use a diamond grinder to mechanically abrade the concrete surface
  • We fill in any cracks or chips in the concrete
  • We apply a base coat of epoxy
  • We apply epoxy with bauder metallic
  • We add a clear top coat to finish off the process (Epoxy or Polyaspartic).
